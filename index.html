what we offer

Examinations and Diagnosis

We diagnose more specific health concerns and offer in-house diagnosis options. We also carry out checkups which focuses on aspects of your pet’s general wellness. For example:
  • Coat
  • Skin
  • Teeth
  • Temperature
  • Behavioural patterns
  • Diet

Vaccines

Vaccination of your pet(s) is the most vital first steps as a pet owner. Vaccines protect your pet from a variety of conditions:
  • Rabies
  • Lyme disease
  • Panleukopenia
  • Parvovirus
  • Calicivirus
  • Distemper
You can prevent costly future medical treatments for your animal and protect your family from the side effects of these illnesses. This is especially important for conditions like rabies.
